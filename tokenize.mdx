---
title: "Square Tokenizer"
description: "Tokenize a card via Square PCI and get a nonce + verification token."

Tokenize a card through Square's PCI endpoint. Returns a card nonce, card info, and buyer verification token for Square Online checkout.

## Request

</ParamField>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Session initialization">
    A new payment session is created against the Square Online site using
    the provided application ID and location ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Card tokenization via Square PCI">
    The card details are submitted to Square's PCI-compliant tokenization
    endpoint, which returns a single-use card nonce.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Card verification and BIN lookup">
    The card nonce is validated and a BIN lookup is performed to determine
    the card brand, last four digits, and expiration date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3D Secure verification">
    If required, a 3D Secure challenge is processed to verify the
    cardholder identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buyer verification token generated">
    A buyer verification token is generated and returned alongside the
    card nonce. Both are single-use and ready for checkout submission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Errors

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="400 -- Bad Request">
    Missing or invalid parameters. Check that all required fields (`cc`, `mm`,
    `yyyy`, `cvv`, `app_id`, `loc_id`, `base_url`) are present and correctly
    formatted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="401 -- Unauthorized">
    Invalid or missing `X-API-Key` header. Verify your API key is correct
    and active.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="429 -- Rate Limited">
    Too many requests. You have exceeded your rate limit. Wait before
    retrying or upgrade your plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="500 -- Internal Server Error">
    Tokenization failed upstream. The card may be invalid, the Square
    application ID or location ID may be incorrect, or Square's PCI
    endpoint may be temporarily unavailable. Retry or check your inputs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Typical response time is 5-15 seconds. Both `card_nonce` and
  `verification_token` are single-use -- they cannot be reused across
  multiple checkout attempts. The `app_id` and `loc_id` values are
  site-specific and must match the target Square Online store.
</Info>
